Getting there

I'm flying!


[PVD] Rhode Island T.F. Green International Airport


Drive or carpool ~30min.
Ferry ~1.5hr from Downtown Providence Ferry Terminal. ($12)
Bus ~1.5hr [Line 14] from the airport. ($2)

[BOS] Boston Logan International Airport


Drive or carpool ~1hr 15min
Take the train (the T) (or a short cab) to South Station, and Dattco shuttles can take you to Newport ($16)

I'm taking the train!
